Prospective Students

We welcome motivated SJSU graduate and undergraduate students interested in autonomous systems, multi-robot path planning, optimization, or applied machine learning. If your interests align with the lab’s research, please email maryam.khazaei@sjsu.edu with your CV and a short note describing what you would like to work on. Undergraduate research opportunities — including the Advanced AI Certificate research internship program — are also available periodically.
