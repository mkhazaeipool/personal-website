Prospective Students

We welcome motivated SJSU graduate students interested in autonomous systems, multi-robot path planning, optimization, or applied machine learning. If your interests align with the lab’s research, please email maryam.khazaei@sjsu.edu with your CV and a short note describing what you would like to work on. Undergraduate research opportunities are also available periodically.
